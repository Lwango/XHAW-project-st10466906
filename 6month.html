Course Overview

The 6-month courses at Alfredo learning center provide students with comprehensive, career-oriented training. Each program is designed to equip learners with both theoretical and practical skills, preparing them for employment or small business opportunities in their chosen field.

These courses cover essential topics such as personal development, hands-on practice, and safety procedures, ensuring a well-rounded education experience.
